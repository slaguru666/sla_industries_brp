# NPCs & Adversaries (BRP – SLA Industries)

Document Status: First Draft – Playable  
Scope: Fast-building enemies and contacts for SLA BRP, keyed to BPN colour and Mort threats.

---

## 1. NPC Tiers – Mooks to Monsters

Use tiers so you do not overbuild every stat block.

- Tier 1 – Extras
  - Downtown gangers, civilians, basic security, background Shivers.
  - One or two useful skills, low HP, go down fast.
- Tier 2 – Standard Opposition
  - DarkNight cells, competent gangers, serial killers, proper Shiver squads.
  - Wider skill set, decent gear, dangerous in groups.
- Tier 3 – Elite Operatives
  - DarkNight specialists, Thresher powersuits, contract killers, Internal/Cloak agents.
  - High skills, strong armour, special action economy pressure.
- Tier 4 – Horrors and Set-Piece Threats
  - Carrien packs, Manchines, Necanthropic entities.
  - Break normal assumptions: fear pressure, extra attacks, regeneration, brutal armour.

---

## 2. Standard NPC Stat Block

For all NPCs, use:

- Name and Role
- Tier
- Attributes: STR, CON, DEX, INT, POW, CHA, SIZ
- HP
- Key Skills (only list skills you will roll)
- Attacks (skill %, damage, notes)
- Armour
- Traits
- Gear
- BPN Hooks (where this opposition appears)

Target 6-10 practical lines for table speed.

## 4. SLA-Flavoured Trait Library

Apply one or two to instantly SLA-ify an adversary:

- Media Darling: social leverage while cameras are live.
- Corporate Asset: death/escalation creates Grey BPN fallout.
- Addict: temporary edge with crash risk.
- Obsessive: cannot disengage without POW pressure.
- Internal Watchlisted: actions around this NPC affect Internal heat.

## 7. Table-Speed Guidance

- Use Tier 1 in groups and let them fold quickly.
- Use Tier 2 as core pressure pieces.
- Use Tier 3 sparingly for command-level stakes.
- Use Tier 4 for scene-defining terror, not routine filler.

If a fight drags, increase consequences not hit points.
